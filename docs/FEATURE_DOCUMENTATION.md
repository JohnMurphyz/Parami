## 1. Daily Parami Wisdom

### Overview
Each day, all users worldwide see the same Parami (Buddhist perfection) with teachings, quotes, and practical guidance.

### Steps to Use

1. **Open the app**
   - The Journey tab opens by default

2. **View today's Parami**
   - See the Parami name (e.g., "DƒÅna - Generosity")
   - Read the full description explaining the perfection
   - Scroll to see an inspiring quote from Buddhist teachings

3. **Read practice suggestions**
   - Three context-specific practices appear below the quote
   - Each practice is numbered and tagged by context (work, home, relationships, etc.)
   - Practices range from easy to challenging difficulty

4. **Mark practices as complete**
   - Tap the checkbox next to any practice you complete
   - The practice gets a checkmark and moves to your history

5. **View previous days**
   - Scroll down on the Journey tab to see "Your Journey"
   - Tap any past date card to expand and see that day's Parami and practices

### Key Features
- Universal rotation: Everyone sees the same Parami daily
- Seeded randomization ensures variety without repetition
- 10 Paramis rotate throughout the days
- Practice completion tracking

## 2. Quick Journal Entry

### Overview
Capture brief reflections, thoughts, or notes about your daily practice.

### Steps to Use

1. **Open the Journey tab**

2. **Locate the Daily Reflection card**
   - At the top of the screen
   - Shows "Daily Reflection" with Parami logo

3. **Expand the card (if collapsed)**
   - Tap the card to expand
   - Shows "Quick Entry" and "Deep Reflection" tabs

4. **Select "Quick Entry" mode**
   - Tap the "Quick Entry" tab (should be selected by default)

5. **Write your entry**
   - Tap in the text area: "Begin writing..."
   - Type your reflection (max 2,000 characters)
   - Character count appears at the bottom

6. **Submit your entry**
   - Tap the "Submit" button
   - Entry is saved automatically
   - Card collapses showing "Quick Entry Captured" badge

7. **View your entries**
   - Tap "Entries" card in "Your Progress" section
   - All journal entries appear in chronological order

### Key Features
- Simple, unstructured writing
- Auto-saves on submission
- Max 2,000 characters
- Character counter displayed
- Quick access from Journey tab

## 3. Deep Reflection System

### Overview
A comprehensive 7-section guided spiritual reflection practice based on Buddhist teachings. Tracks ego patterns, mental cultivation, resilience, and emotional states.

### The Five Spiritual Components

This system is based on five Buddhist contemplative practices:

1. **The Ego Audit** - Unmasking self-deception (Three Lords of Materialism)
2. **The Garden Log** - Tracking mental seed cultivation (wholesome vs unwholesome)
3. **The Nutriment Audit** - Examining your mental diet (four nutriments)
4. **The Vicissitudes of Life** - Resilience to worldly conditions (8 pairs)
5. **The Chariot of Disappointment** - Relationship with spiritual expectations

### Steps to Complete a Deep Reflection

#### Starting the Reflection

1. **Open the Journey tab**

2. **Locate the Daily Reflection card**

3. **Select "Deep Reflection" mode**
   - Tap the "Deep Reflection" tab in the mode switcher

4. **Tap "Begin Deep Reflection"**
   - Opens full-screen guided modal
   - Shows progress indicator: "Section 1 of 7"

#### Section 1: The Ego Audit (3-5 minutes)

**Purpose**: Identify how ego hijacks spiritual progress

1. **Three Lords of Materialism**
   - Check boxes for any that appeared today:
     - ‚ñ° Lord of Form (seeking neurotic comfort)
     - ‚ñ° Lord of Speech (using intellect as shield)
     - ‚ñ° Lord of Mind (using spirituality to feel special)
   - Add notes about which lords appeared

2. **Spiritual Advisor**
   - Answer: "What rationalizations did you catch yourself making today?"
   - Type in the text field

3. **"Are You Sure?"**
   - Answer: "What perceptions did you challenge today? Describe a moment you questioned your version of reality."
   - Type your response

4. **Tap "Next"** to continue
   - Auto-saves your progress

#### Section 2: The Garden Log (3-5 minutes)

**Purpose**: Track which mental seeds were nurtured

1. **Wholesome Seeds Watered**
   - Tap chips to select all that apply:
   - Generosity, Patience, Mindfulness, Joy, Compassion, Gratitude, Loving-kindness, Peace, Wisdom, Effort

2. **Unwholesome Seeds Watered**
   - Tap chips to select all that apply:
   - Anger, Jealousy, Craving, Aversion, Fear, Anxiety, Pride, Delusion, Resentment, Greed

3. **Changing the Peg**
   - Answer: "Describe a moment when you successfully replaced an unwholesome thought with a wholesome one."

4. **"Hello, Habit Energy"**
   - Answer: "When did you practice 'mere recognition' today? (Simply acknowledging a pattern without judgment)"

5. **Tap "Next"**

#### Section 3: The Nutriment Audit (5-7 minutes)

**Purpose**: Examine the four nutriments that sustained consciousness today

1. **Edible Food**
   - Toggle: "Was your eating mindful today?" (Yes/No)
   - Add notes about your eating experience

2. **Sense Impressions**
   - List toxic media, conversations, or images consumed
   - Describe how these sense impressions affected you

3. **Intention (Volition)**
   - Describe your deep, driving desire today
   - Select motivation: Self-gain | Welfare of others | Both

4. **Consciousness (Collective Energy)**
   - Describe what collective energies influenced your mind today

5. **Tap "Next"**

#### Section 4: The Vicissitudes of Life (5-7 minutes)

**Purpose**: Track reactions to the 8 worldly conditions

1. **For each of the 8 worldly conditions:**
   - Gain/Loss
   - Fame/Disrepute
   - Praise/Blame
   - Pleasure/Pain

2. **If a condition occurred:**
   - Tap to expand the card
   - Toggle "Did this occur today?" to Yes
   - Describe how you reacted

3. **The Second Arrow**
   - Toggle: "Did you add mental grief to physical pain today?"
   - If yes, describe the second arrow (mental resentment, anxiety, etc.)

4. **Tap "Next"**

#### Section 5: The Chariot of Disappointment (3-4 minutes)

**Purpose**: Track relationship with spiritual expectations

1. **Practice Feeling Tedious**
   - Toggle: "Did your practice feel tedious, unsuccessful, or unmagical today?"

2. **Hard Ground Moments**
   - Describe times today when you accepted reality as it is (landing on hard ground)

3. **Soft Landing Attempts**
   - Describe times when you sought spiritual fantasy or comfort (preparing a soft landing)

4. **Tap "Next"**

#### Section 6: Daily Contemplations (5-7 minutes)

**Purpose**: Reflect on four guiding questions

Answer these four contemplations:

1. **Self-Reliance**
   - "Did I act as my own master today, or did I seek an external 'savior'?"

2. **Nowness**
   - "How many times did I 'stop the horse' of habit to dwell in the present moment?"

3. **Non-Attachment**
   - "Which of my 'cows' (possessions or ideas) did I practice releasing today?"

4. **Clarity**
   - "Did I look at my mind as I look at my face in a mirror‚Äîwithout judgment?"

5. **Tap "Next"**

#### Section 7: Reflection Summary (3-5 minutes)

**Purpose**: Complete your reflection with emotional state and closing thoughts

1. **Select your emotional state** (tap one):
   - üòå Peaceful
   - üôè Grateful
   - üí™ Challenged
   - üò∞ Restless
   - üòî Discouraged

2. **Select your resilience level** (tap one):
   - Stable
   - Wavering
   - Struggling

3. **Overall Reflection**
   - Write a summary of your day or add closing reflections
   - **Required field** - must have content to submit

4. **Read the encouragement**
   - "You Don't Walk Alone" - reminder about spiritual friendship
   - Special message if you marked yourself as struggling

5. **Tap "Submit Reflection"**
   - Confirmation dialog appears
   - Reflection is saved
   - Modal closes

### Navigation & Progress

- **Progress bar** at top shows: "42% Complete"
- **Back button** available (except on first section)
- **Close button** (X) - saves progress, can resume later
- **Auto-save** happens after each section

### Key Features

## 6. The Crossing Over Diagnostic

### Overview
A comprehensive quiz that assesses your strength in each of the 10 Paramis. Helps identify which perfections are strong and which need attention.

### Steps to Take the Quiz

1. **Access the quiz**
   - From Journey tab, find "Discover Your Path" section
   - Tap "The Crossing Over Diagnostic" card
   - First time: Shows "Discover which paramis..."
   - Subsequent times: Shows "See your past results..."

2. **Begin the diagnostic**
   - Welcome screen appears
   - Read introduction about the 10 Paramis
   - Tap "Begin Diagnostic" button

3. **Answer 10 questions** (one per Parami)

   For each question:
   - Read the scenario carefully
   - Four response options presented
   - Each option represents a different approach
   - Select the response that resonates most
   - Progress bar shows: "Question X of 10"
   - Tap "Next" to continue

4. **Complete all questions**
   - Answer all 10 scenarios
   - Each tests a different Parami
   - No wrong answers - just honest reflection

5. **View your results**
   - Radar chart shows all 10 Paramis
   - Scores displayed (0-100 for each)
   - Color-coded by strength:
     - Green: Strong (70-100)
     - Orange: Moderate (40-69)
     - Pink: Needs attention (0-39)

6. **Read your insights**
   - Overall assessment card
   - Strongest Paramis listed (top 3)
   - Weakest Paramis listed (bottom 3)
   - Personalized guidance for each

7. **Review individual Paramis**
   - Scroll through all 10 Parami cards
   - Each shows:
     - Score (0-100)
     - Strength level (Strong/Moderate/Needs Attention)
     - Description of that Parami
     - Specific advice for cultivating it

8. **Save or retake**
   - Results automatically saved
   - Can retake anytime from quiz history
   - Tap "Done" to return

### Accessing Past Results

1. **From Journey tab**
   - Tap "The Crossing Over Diagnostic" card
   - Shows "Last taken: [date]"

2. **Opens quiz history**
   - Lists all past attempts
   - Most recent at top
   - Shows date and overall score

3. **Tap any result**
   - Opens full results for that attempt
   - View radar chart
   - Review insights
   - Compare with other attempts

### Key Features

- 10 scenario-based questions
- Radar chart visualization
- Color-coded strength levels
- Personalized insights and advice
- History tracking
- Retake capability
- Progress tracking over time

## 9. Daily Notifications

### Overview
Receive daily reminders at a customizable time to check today's Parami and reflect.

### Steps to Enable Notifications

1. **First Launch Setup**
   - During onboarding, notification permission requested
   - Tap "Allow" to enable
   - If denied, can enable later in Settings

2. **Configure Notification Time**
   - Go to Settings tab
   - Find "Notification Settings" section
   - Toggle "Daily Notifications" to ON
   - Tap "Notification Time"
   - Use time picker to select preferred time
   - Default: 9:00 AM
   - Tap "Save" or "Done"

3. **Receive Notifications**
   - Daily notification arrives at scheduled time
   - Shows:
     - Today's Parami name
     - Short description
     - App icon
   - Tap notification to open app to today's Parami

### Steps to Modify Notifications

1. **Change time**
   - Settings ‚Üí Notification Time
   - Pick new time
   - Save

2. **Disable notifications**
   - Settings ‚Üí Toggle "Daily Notifications" OFF
   - Stops all notifications

3. **Re-enable notifications**
   - Settings ‚Üí Toggle "Daily Notifications" ON
   - May need to re-grant OS permission

### Notification Features

- **Daily delivery**: One notification per day
- **Customizable time**: Any time, 24-hour format
- **Smart content**: Shows today's Parami info
- **Deep linking**: Opens app to correct screen
- **Local notifications**: No internet required
- **Battery efficient**: Minimal impact

### Troubleshooting

**Not receiving notifications:**
1. Check Settings ‚Üí Daily Notifications is ON
2. Check device Settings ‚Üí Parami ‚Üí Notifications allowed
3. Ensure Do Not Disturb is not blocking
4. Try disabling and re-enabling in app Settings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Do I need internet to use the app?**
A: No. All features work offline. Only initial content download requires internet.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. All data stored locally on your device. No cloud sync, no data collection.

**Q: Can I use this without being Buddhist?**
A: Absolutely. The teachings are universal and applicable to anyone interested in mindfulness and self-awareness.

### Reflections

**Q: How long does a deep reflection take?**
A: 30-45 minutes for thorough completion. Can be done in multiple sessions.

**Q: Can I skip sections in deep reflection?**
A: Yes, but completing all sections provides best insights for analytics.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: No problem. You can do reflections anytime. Analytics work with whatever data you provide.

### Analytics

**Q: How many reflections needed for analytics?**
A: Minimum 1 to see analytics, but 3+ recommended for meaningful trends.

**Q: Can I export my analytics?**
A: Not in current version. Future enhancement planned.

**Q: Do analytics judge my practice?**
A: No. They're mirrors for awareness, not judgments. All patterns are part of the path.

### Technical

**Q: Why isn't my notification working?**
A: Check device Settings ‚Üí Parami ‚Üí Notifications. Ensure permissions granted.

**Q: Can I sync across devices?**
A: Not in current version. Data stays local to each device.

**Q: What if I accidentally delete a reflection?**
A: Current version doesn't have deletion. All entries are preserved.
